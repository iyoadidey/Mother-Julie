Mother Julie

"Tastes like no other"

The very first Filipino ice dessert brand that aims to produce products that are not excessively sweet. It was established in the heart of Barrio Menu, located in Tondo, Manila, Philippines. We are well-known for delivering original and high-quality iced desserts, iced coffees, specialty drinks, and food.

Our journey began in Tondo, Manila, along Old Torres 1st Street. What started in 1983 grew through passion, family traditions, and dedication — and on November 25, 2024, we officially opened our first store to the community.

Founder

Bee Bernabe, a young and talented entrepreneur and business consultant, invented a dessert that suits the taste of Filipinos who prefer desserts that are not overly sweet. Filipinos are known for their love of saccharine treats and iced coffees.

It all started with a famous panghimagas (dessert) in the Philippines called Mais Con Yelo (Iced Milk Corn). From there, Bee took the lead in innovating the business by introducing new flavors and eventually offering iced coffees, specialty drinks, and food.

Today, Mother Julie's store has opened another branch on Perfecto Street, Manila, on November 25, 2025 — exactly a year after its anniversary.
